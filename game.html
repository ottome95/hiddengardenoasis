About


In A Nutshell

In the hustle and bustle of fast-paced Manhattan, it can be hard to find any sense of peace and serenity. It seems everywhere you turn, there's another skyscraper, crowded streets and no trees and grass to be found. Where can one go to find a bit of nature and quiet that's off the beaten path? We all know about busy, crowd-drawing parks like Central Park, Washington Square Park and Union Square parks, but what about other less known places?

Chances are, if you looked hard and in some unlikely places, there are some public gardens hidden in plain sight that you can retreat to for some zen and calmness. There's no need to plan a getaway out of the city. Perhaps in the middle of your busy day, whether for lunch or a java break, you can step inside one of these sanctuaries and enjoy a little bit of tranquility.

Let us take you on a tour of these hidden gems! Browse the photos of these gardens in our gallery and refer to the map to find these exact spots!
